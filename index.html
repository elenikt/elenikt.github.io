Eleni Koutsiona

Master’s Student in Astrophysics

Hello! My name is Eleni. I come from Greece and I did my Bachelor's in Physics at the University of Crete, before moving to Germany where I am currently pursuing a Master's in Astrophysics at Heidelberg University. My research so far has mostly been focused on Luminous Infrared Galaxies, but I am also interested in transient astronomy, black holes, and applications of machine learning in astrophysics.

🌌 Research Interests

  • Luminous Infrared Galaxies (LIRGs)
  • Supernovae & transient phenomena
  • Black Holes
  • Dust attenuation
  • Machine Learning in Astrophysics
